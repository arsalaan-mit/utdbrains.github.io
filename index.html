Overview

UTD Brains is an artificial intelligence research group at The University of Texas at Dallas that focuses on cutting-edge machine learning research and publication. Our team works on advancing the state-of-the-art in AI/ML through innovative research projects and collaborations. We welcome students and researchers from all backgrounds who are passionate about artificial intelligence. Our group meets regularly to discuss recent AI/ML papers and advance our own research initiatives. Our goal is to foster an inclusive, collaborative environment where members can gain hands-on research experience and contribute to the advancement of AI technology.

Research Focus

Computer Vision, Neurosymbolic AI, Audio Processing, and Multimodal Learning
